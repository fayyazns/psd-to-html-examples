OUR MISSION

WHEN IT COMES TO HAZARD... SPEED, ACCURACY, DETAILS, TIMING AND WHO DELIVERS MATTERS.

Flood Matters produces automated and semi-automated Flood Determination Reports. We offer a superior product in a timely manner via automation or connection with Loan Servicing Systems that beats the competition in accuracy and will be delivered instantaneously for 95% of the properties in the nation. We feel you can make the best informed decisions when you have accurate information concerning the property you are inquiring about.

WHY CHOOSE HAZARD MATTERS?

Flood Matters takes pride in providing the newest and most effective tools for clients. In today’s service industry of this market space most are still relying on a lot of manual labor to complete the reports. There are also very few integrations with Loan Origination and Loan Service Systems, which requires on or off shore resources on the target customer’s side. Automation is becoming more and more relevant in this industry, as well as, compliance when it comes to privacy, vetting out vendors and products and streamlined services.

AT HAZARD MATTERS, ACCURACY IS A PRIORITY.

Flood Matters provides the most comprehensive, fastest, easy-to-read and accurate Flood Determination Reports available to the real estate industry. Most reports can be delivered within seconds (qualified areas) which would help lead on closing transactions quickly with the accurate information in hand.
